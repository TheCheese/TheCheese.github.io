Raptor Pest Control

Raptor Pest Control is a family owned and operated company specialising in all facets of pest management. Our customer base includes both commercial properties and residential homes.

We understand everyone is different and we pride ourselves on listening to our clients needs and offering tailor made solutions specifically designed to suit your requirements. Whether it be termites, bed bugs, rodents, spiders, ants, cockroaches or any of the others pests that invade our homes and businesses, Raptor Pest Control has the solution.

Raptor Pest Control are continually evaluating our products and procedures to ensure we offer the most environmentally friendly and consumer safe options for the treatment at hand and are more than happy to discuss the various options with you.

All of Raptor Pest Controls technicians must pass the state required testing and training criteria, and must undergo a rigorous company training curriculum prior to being Raptor Pest Control certified. Our pest control technicians also meet monthly for continuing education courses to stay up-to-date with seasonal trends and new pest management methods.
