Ant Control

Integra Termite and Pest Control understands what a nuisance ants can be to your family, property, and business. With over 23 years experience in providing ant control to the Fort Worth area, we can diagnose and eliminate the problem quickly and safely.

Whether it’s fire ants, carpenter ants, or any other variety – you can rely on our experience and dedication to meet your needs. All of our pest control technicians are highly trained and certified, and we use the most effective treatments that are guaranteed to be safe for use around people and pets.

Carpenter Ants

Carpenter ants build nests inside wood that has been chewed out by their mandibles. Unlike termites, they do not eat the wood. Sometimes carpenter ants will hollow out sections of trees, and are also known for infesting wooden buildings and structures. They are a supreme nuisance and major cause of structural damage.
